How to Use Your Own Handwriting

Transform your personal handwriting into a digital font with these simple steps:

  1. Create Your Font: Visit Calligraphr to convert your handwriting into a font file. Follow their guided process to create a custom .ttf or .otf font file.
  2. Upload Your Font: Once you have your font file, click the "Upload your handwriting font" button in the Handwriting Options section above.
  3. Generate: Your text will now appear in your personal handwriting style. Adjust spacing and size as needed for the best results.

Note: Creating a custom font may take 15-30 minutes, but the result is a truly personalized handwriting experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my own handwriting?

You'll need to create a font file from your handwriting. Check out our detailed guide above for step-by-step instructions on using Calligraphr to create and upload your custom font.

Where can I find additional handwriting fonts?

You can download free handwriting fonts from Quantum Enterprises Font Vault. Make sure to download .ttf or .otf format files for compatibility.

Why are there gaps between letters in my custom font?

Letter spacing can vary depending on how the font was created. Use the "Letter Spacing" and "Word Spacing" controls in the Spacing Options section to adjust the gaps and achieve the desired appearance.

Can I use this for commercial purposes?

Yes, Handwriter is open-source and free to use. However, please ensure that any custom fonts you upload have appropriate licensing for your intended use.

How do I report bugs or request features?

Visit our GitHub Issues page to report bugs or suggest new features. Please provide detailed information to help us understand and address your request.
